Are application fee waivers available?

Yes, we understand the financial constraints students may face. We offer application fee waivers based on certain criteria. Eligible candidates can apply for a fee waiver by submitting the required documentation along with their application.

How can I request a fee waiver?

To request a fee waiver, please visit our official website and navigate to the admissions section. You'll find detailed instructions on the fee waiver application process. Make sure to review the eligibility criteria and provide all necessary documents for a smooth evaluation process.

What are the common reasons for fee waiver approval?

Fee waivers are typically approved for candidates facing financial hardship, individuals receiving government assistance, or those participating in specific scholarship programs. Each request is carefully reviewed, and decisions are based on the provided documentation and adherence to the eligibility criteria.

Is there a limit on the number of fee waiver requests?

No, there is no strict limit on the number of fee waiver requests. However, it's crucial to note that each request is individually assessed. Meeting the eligibility criteria and providing accurate documentation significantly increases the chances of a successful fee waiver approval.
